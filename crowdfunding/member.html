Members of


Singapore International Chamber of Commerce (SICC)

Their website

SICC is a wholly independent, non-profit business association. It listens to the concerns of partnering businesses and represents them to the government. It’s goal is to achieve greater mutual understanding and practical solutions.

Singapore Infocomm Technology Federation (SITF)

Their website

SITF is Singapore’s infocomm industry association, comprising of corporate members ranging from local startups to establish MNCs. It advocates the ICM (Information, Communications and Media) industry and helps to accelerate the adoption ICM technology.

Singapore Business Federation (SBF)

Their website

SBF is the apex business chamber that champions the interests of the Singapore business community in trade, investment and industrial relations. Primarily, it acts as the bridge between the business community and government in Singapore to create and enhance a conducive business environment.

Singapore Chinese Chamber of Commerce (SCCCI)

Their website

SCCCI is an internationally renowned business organisation and the apex body of the Chinese business community in Singapore. It comprises of 4,000 corporate members and over 150 trade association members, representing over 40,000 companies. It plays a key and pro-active role in representing the interests of the local business community.

Association of Small and Medium Enterprises (ASME)

Their website

ASME is a not-for-profit organisation, for entrepreneurs by entrepreneurs. With wide-ranging services and programmes, ASME strives to equip member SMEs with the business knowledge and market opportunities to help them grow their businesses.
